Know Your Rights

For Immigrants

  • You have the right to remain silent.
  • You don’t have to open the door to ICE without a signed warrant.

For Trappers

  • Never consent to a search. Say "I don’t consent to a search."
  • Ask if you’re being detained. If not, calmly walk away.

For Racers

  • Street racing can lead to fines, jail time, and license suspension.
  • Keep footage off social media—it can and will be used in court.

Know Your Rights

Legal tips for immigrants, trappers, racers & more.

  • Don’t consent to searches — ask if you’re free to go.
  • Remain silent — you have that right.
  • Ask for a lawyer before answering questions.
  • Record if pulled over — it’s legal in most states.
  • Trappers — know the local distribution laws.
  • Racers — understand reckless driving & impoundment laws.
  • Immigrants — never sign ICE documents without legal advice.

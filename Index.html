Know Your Rights

Immigrants, Minorities, Everyone

  • You have the right to remain silent.
  • You do NOT have to consent to a search.
  • Ask if you're being detained or are free to go.

For Hustlers, Trappers, and Racers

  • Never self-snitch online or in texts. Move in silence.
  • High-speed racing laws vary, but many include felonies & vehicle seizures. Know your local code.
  • NEVER give consent to a search. Always say, “I don’t consent to any searches.”
